  • Homemade Choc Cookies

  • Choc Cookies are great for many occassions. Christmas... Or just on any days

    Its made up of simple basic ingredients, so anyone can bake cookies :)

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 Cup Butter (melted)
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 Cup Flour (Might Need More)
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • choc chips & mix of nuts

Instructions

  • - Melt Butter & Pre-heat oven to 180c- How to
  • - Add sugar, salt, butter to a bowl. Mix
  • - Add egg, baking soda. Mix again
  • - Add flour gradually
  • - Add Choc Chip and nuts
  • - Shape to a desired size ball
  • - Bake at 180c for 15 minutes

Ukrainian refugees speak of bombs, half-empty cities, hunger

Yulia Bondarieva spent 10 days in a basement as Russian planes flew over and bombs were falling on Kharkiv


By SRDJAN NEDELJKOVIC and DAVID KEYTON Associated Press

22 March 2022, 09:23 • 5 min read


MEDYKA, Poland -- Yulia Bondarieva spent 10 days in a basement as Russian planes
flew over and bombs were falling on the Ukrainian city of Kharkiv. Having reached safety in Poland,
Bondarieva's only wish now is for her twin sister in the besieged city of Mariupol to get out, too.


“They have been in the basement since Feb. 24, they have not been out at all,” Bondarieva said. “They
are running out of food and water.”


Bondarieva, 24, managed to speak to her sister on the phone recently. The fear of what will happen to
her in the encircled and bombed-out city that is going through some of the worst fighting in the war
has been overwhelming.


“She does not know how to leave the city,” Bondarieva said after arriving in the Polish border town of Medyka.

  • Ice Cream Latte Boba

  • Coffee drinks are my favourite, combined with tapioca pearls make them even better.

    Ice-cream latte is pretty much a regular latte with a scoop of icecream.

    Other flavours could work too but I prefer vanilla/milk ice-cream for the bets flavours.

    This is a very simple drink to make, and using dairy alternatives are a great idea.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Milk/vanilla Ice-cream
  • Tapioca Pearls
  • espresso shot
  • milk
  • ice

Instructions

  • - Prepare Tapioca Pearls - How to
  • - Add a desired amount of tapioca pearls to a cup
  • - Add a scoop of icecream
  • - Pour milk
  • - Pour espresso shot
  • - Enjoy!
  • - Using dairy-free milk also makes a great drink!

  • Blue Latte Bubble Tea

  • Blue latte, or butterfly pea latte, is a very interesting drink, with a natural blue colour.

    butterfly pea tea leaves are naturaly blue, and it can be used for special drinks.

    This tea gives off an earthy leafy flavour, and can help with brightning moods.

    Because the tea is high in alkali, the tea reacts with acidity and turns purple.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup watermelon
  • Tapioca Pearls
  • a touch honey
  • ice (optional)

Instructions

  • - Prepare Tapioca Pearls - How to
  • - Add a desired amount of tapioca pearls to a cup
  • - Blend the watermelons and a touch of honey
  • - Add ice (optional)
  • - Pour juice
  • - Enjoy!
  • - Always serve cold!

Strawberry Smoothie Bubble Tea - Frutta Boba

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Frozen Strawberries
  • Milk 3/4 Cup
  • 1 TBSP Honey (optional)
  • tapioca pearls

Instructions

  1. - Boil tapioca pearls for 20 minutes, turn off heat, keep lid on for 5 minutes - How to
  2. - Drain water and rinse under cold water
  3. - Add brown sugar syrup, and boil for 5 minutes. Make sure to stir
  4. - Add pearls to a cup (to your likings)
  5. - Add Ice and pour milk
  6. - Enjoy!
  8. - Adjust the sweetness to your likings by adding more, or less syrup

Tortilla Pizza - Frutta Boba

Ingredients

  1. Tortilla
  2. Tomato sauce
  3. A handful of mozzarella cheese
  5. --Additional Toppings--
  6. Pepperoni
  7. parsley
  8. Etc

Instructions

  1. - Preheat oven to 205C
  2. - Lay parchment paper on pan and lay torilla on top of parchment paper
  3. - Spread tomato suace on tortillla
  4. - Sprinkle a handful of mozzarella cheese
  5. - Add additional toppings (parsley, Pepperoni, etc)
  6. - Bake pizza in oven for 15 minutes (or untill the cheese has metled)
  7. - Take pizza out of oven and cut them into slices of pizza
  8. - Enjoy!

• Brown Sugar Bubble Tea

  • Brown sugar bubble tea originates from taiwan and the drink can now be found world-wide
    Brown sugar bubble tea, 黑糖珍珠奶茶 (Hēitáng zhēnzhū nǎichá) in Chinese, is made up of three key
    ingredients, these are milk, brown sugar syrup, and tapioca pearls.

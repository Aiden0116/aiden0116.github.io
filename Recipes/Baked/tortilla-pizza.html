• Tortilla Pizza

  • Pizza, dish of Itallian orgin is a dish enjoyed world-wide. This dish mainly consists

    of dough, tomato sauce, and cheese. But there are many varieties of pizzas

    containing different varieties of ingredients. Pizza's can be time consuming to make

    so I sometimes like to replace the dough with tortillas.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Tomato sauce
  • A handful of mozzarella cheese
  • --Additional Toppings--
  • Pepperoni
  • parsley
  • Etc

Instructions

  • - Preheat oven to 205C
  • - Lay parchment paper on pan and lay torilla on top of parchment paper
  • - Spread tomato suace on tortillla
  • - Sprinkle a handful of mozzarella cheese
  • - Add additional toppings (parsley, Pepperoni, etc)
  • - Bake pizza in oven for 15 minutes (or untill the cheese has metled)
  • - Take pizza out of oven and cut them into slices of pizza
  • - Enjoy!

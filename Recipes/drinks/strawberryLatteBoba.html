  • Strawberry Latte Boba

  • Strawberry Latte, or strawberry milk is a very popular in South korea.

    Unlike traditional strawberry milk, Korean strawberry latte is composed of

    chunky strawberry puree, and milk. This drink is always served cold.

    This is a very tasty drink, and it also works brillantly with bubble tea.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Strawberry Puree
  • Tapioca Pearls
  • milk
  • ice

Instructions

  • - Prepare Tapioca Pearls - How to
  • - Add a desired amount of tapioca pearls to a cup
  • - Add strawberry puree (to your likings
  • - Add ice
  • - Pour Milk (to your likings)
  • - Enjoy!
  • - Using dairy-free milk also makes a great drink!

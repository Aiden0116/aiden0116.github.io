• Blueberry Smoothie Bubble Tea

  • Bubble tea's, also known as boba is a tea based drink with chewy tapioca pearls.

    Overtime, more varieties of bubbles were created, some with tea, and some without.

    The cold, andrefreshing smoothie varient makes a great summer drink.

    One of my favourite smoothie flavours is blueberry

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Frozen blueberries
  • 3/4 Cup Milk
  • 1 TBSP Honey (optional)
  • Tapioca pearls

Instructions

  • - Boil tapioca pearls for 20 minutes, turn off heat, keep lid on for 5 minutes - How to
  • - Drain water and rinse under cold water
  • - Sweeten pearls with brown sugar
  • - Blend blueberry and milk in a belnder (you can also add a TBSP of honey here)
  • - Add pearls to a cup (to your likings)
  • - Pour smoothie (add whipped cream if you would like to)
  • - Enjoy!
  • - Adjust sweetness by adding more or less honey

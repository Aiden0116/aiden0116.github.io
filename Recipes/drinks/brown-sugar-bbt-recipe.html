  • Brown Sugar Bubble Tea

  • Brown sugar bubble tea originates from Taiwan and the drink can now be found world-wide

    Brown sugar bubble tea, 黑糖珍珠奶茶 (Hēitáng zhēnzhū nǎichá) in Chinese, is made up of three key

    ingredients, these are milk, brown sugar syrup, and tapioca pearls.

    Brown sugar bubble tea originates from Taiwan and the drink can now be found world-wide.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Syrup 1:1 muscovado sugar and water
  • Milk 400ml
  • ice
  • tapioca pearls

Instructions

  • - Boil tapioca pearls for 20 minutes, turn off heat, keep lid on for 5 minutes - How to
  • - Drain water and rinse under cold water
  • - Add brown sugar syrup, and boil for 5 minutes. Make sure to stir
  • - Add pearls to a cup (to your likings)
  • - Add Ice and pour milk
  • - Enjoy!
  • - Adjust the sweetness to your likings by adding more, or less syrup
